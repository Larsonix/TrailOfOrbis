## 1.0.0 - ModJam Release

The initial public release of Trail of Orbis.

- 6 Elemental Attributes with 30 unique stats
- 11-Step Damage Pipeline
- 4 Ailments : Burn, Freeze, Shock, Poison
- 7 Gear Rarities with 101 Modifier definitions
- Quality System 1-101
- 25 Consumable Stones across 7 categories
- 485-Node Skill Tree with 15 regions
- Skill Sanctum 3D instance world
- 14 Realm Biomes (13 combat + 1 utility)
- 4 Realm Sizes with scaling mob counts and rewards
- 13 Map Modifiers (7 difficulty + 6 reward)
- 5-Tier Mob Classification with dynamic Elite spawns
- Dirichlet Stat Distribution across 52 mob stat types
- Death Recap with full damage breakdown
- Loot Filter configurable by Rarity, Type, Quality
- Ancient Gateways with 7 upgrade tiers
- Full UI System : Stats, Attributes, Skill Sanctum
