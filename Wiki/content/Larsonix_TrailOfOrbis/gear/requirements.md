# Gear Requirements

Higher-level and higher-rarity gear has requirements. You can't equip items far above your current power level. This keeps progression meaningful without creating frustrating gates.

---

## Level Requirements

Gear requirements scale with both **item level** and **rarity**. Higher rarity items demand more from you :

| Rarity | Requirement Level |
|--------|------------------|
| [Common](gear-rarities#common) | Very low - almost no requirement |
| [Uncommon](gear-rarities#uncommon) | Low |
| [Rare](gear-rarities#rare) | Moderate |
| [Epic](gear-rarities#epic) | Noticeable |
| [Legendary](gear-rarities#legendary) | Significant |
| [Mythic](gear-rarities#mythic) | Full requirement |
| [Unique](gear-rarities#unique) | Full requirement |

Low-level items (below Level 5) have no requirements regardless of rarity. You can always equip your first drops.

---

## Attribute Requirements

Some gear has **attribute requirements** that demand specific attribute values before you can equip it. These appear on the tooltip with color-coded text :

- **Green** text means you meet the requirement
- **Red** text means you don't

Attribute requirements tie gear choices to your build. A powerful plate chestpiece might require high [Earth](attributes#earth) attribute, while a magic staff might require high [Water](attributes#water) attribute. This creates natural synergy between your attribute investments and your gear choices.

---

## Requirement Philosophy

Requirements are intentionally accessible. The system prevents extreme level-skipping (a brand new player equipping endgame [Mythic](gear-rarities#mythic) gear) without blocking you from using exciting drops.

> [!NOTE]
> If you are playing content at the appropriate level for a drop, you can almost always equip it. Requirements primarily prevent twinking, which is using high-level gear on a low-level character.

---

## Creative Mode

Players in Creative mode bypass all gear requirements. Useful for testing builds and experimenting with different gear combinations without leveling constraints.
