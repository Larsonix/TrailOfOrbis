# Arms & Regions

Your Skill Tree has **15 regions** : one Core at the center, 6 elemental arms along the cardinal 3D axes, and 8 octant arms in the diagonal spaces. Each region has a distinct archetype and stat focus.

---

## Core

The central origin region. Always allocated, connects to all arms, and gives universal bonuses that benefit any build. Every character starts here.

---

## The 6 Elemental Arms

Each elemental arm extends from the Origin along one of the 6 cardinal 3D directions. They represent pure archetypes. Deep investment in one arm creates a specialist build.

| Arm | Direction | Archetype | Stat Focus |
|-----|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **[Fire](attributes#fire)** | +X (East) | Glass Cannon | Physical damage, charged attacks, crit multiplier |
| **[Water](attributes#water)** | -X (West) | Arcane Mage | Spell damage, mana, energy shield |
| **[Lightning](attributes#lightning)** | +Z (South) | Storm Blitz | Attack speed, move speed, crit chance |
| **[Earth](attributes#earth)** | -Z (North) | Iron Fortress | Max health, armor, health regen, block |
| **[Void](attributes#void)** | +Y (Up) | Life Devourer | Life steal, true damage, *DoT* |
| **[Wind](attributes#wind)** | -Y (Down) | Ghost Ranger | Evasion, accuracy, projectile damage |

> [!NOTE]
> In the Skill Sanctum (3D world), you physically walk toward these directions to reach each arm. [Fire](attributes#fire) is always east, [Void](attributes#void) is always above. The layout is consistent and learnable.

| Component | Count | Role |
|-----------|-------|------|
| Entry | 1 | Gateway from Origin |
| Clusters | 4 x (5 basic + 1 notable) = 24 | Main progression path |
| Synergy nodes | 5 | Scale with your investments |
| Keystones | 2 | Build-defining, endgame |

### Arm Stat Details

| Arm | Keystones Reward | Best For |
|-----|-----------------|----------|
| **Fire** | Extreme offense with tradeoffs | Melee DPS, physical builds |
| **Water** | Sustained casting power | Spell casters, mana-dependent builds |
| **Lightning** | Volume and mobility | Fast-hitting, crit-stacking builds |
| **Earth** | Extreme defense with tradeoffs | Tanks, block builds, survivability |
| **Void** | Damage-based sustain | Life steal, *DoT* builds, vampiric play |
| **Wind** | Ranged avoidance | Ranged DPS, evasion tanks |

## The 8 Octant Arms

Octant arms occupy the **diagonal 3D directions** between the elemental arms. They're hybrid regions. Investing in an octant arm lets you blend stats from adjacent elements without backtracking through the Origin. Each combines the themes of 2-3 adjacent elements.

| Arm | Direction | Archetype | Stat Focus |
|-----|-----------|-----------|------------|
| **Havoc** | +X +Y +Z | Offensive Chaos | Crit, *DoT*, attack speed |
| **Juggernaut** | +X +Y -Z | Unkillable Bruiser | Physical + sustain |
| **Striker** | +X -Y +Z | Speed Assassin | Crit, evasion, attack speed |
| **Warden** | +X -Y -Z | Physical Ranger | Projectiles, block |
| **Warlock** | -X +Y +Z | Dark Caster | Spell damage, *DoT* |
| **Lich** | -X +Y -Z | Tanky Dark Mage | *DoT*, defense, shield |
| **Tempest** | -X -Y +Z | Mobile Mage | Spell, projectile, speed |
| **Sentinel** | -X -Y -Z | Defensive Support | Block, evasion, regen |

> [!TIP]
> Octant arms are the key to hybrid builds. Instead of splitting points between two distant elemental arms, invest in the octant arm that sits between them. You get blended stats with fewer points spent on travel nodes.
