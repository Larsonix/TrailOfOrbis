Found at the end of [Dungeon of Fear II](./dungeon-of-fear-i-i), with 3800 Health. Will be surrounded by several Ancient Dark Titan Constructs; they are very tough to deal with if you are not ready for it.

Drops a single Exit Key for the dungeon's treasure room on death, and provides a [Treasure Bag: Dungeon of Fear II](./treasure-bag-dungeon-of-fear-i-i) to each player that dealt damage.
