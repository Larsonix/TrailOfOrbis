Access this dungeon using a [Fragment: Dungeon of Fear II](./fragment-dungeon-of-fear-i-i) on an [Ancient Gateway](https://hytalewiki.org/w/Ancient_Gateway).

The dungeon has a time limit of 30 minutes. Remaining within after the time limit elapses results in death.

This dungeon has an extra Treasure Room accessible via a Gold Dungeon Key.

Defeat the final boss, [Katherina](./katherina-dungeon-of-fear-i-i) to retrieve the Exit Key and access the dungeon's final Treasure Room to be able to escape.

#### Recommended:
Equipment: Cobalt
Players: 1-2
Plenty of Potions
