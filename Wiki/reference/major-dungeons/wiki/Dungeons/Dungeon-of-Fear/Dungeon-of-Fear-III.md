Access this dungeon using a [Fragment: Dungeon of Fear III](./fragment-dungeon-of-fear-i-i-i) on an [Ancient Gateway](https://hytalewiki.org/w/Ancient_Gateway).

The dungeon has a time limit of 40 minutes. Remaining within after the time limit elapses results in death.

This dungeon has an extra Treasure Room accessible via a Gold Dungeon Key.

There are two Mini-Bosses required to defeat in order to progress as they drop the red Mini-Boss Keys, as well as a Green Key obtained via a Parkour challenge.

Defeat the final boss, [Baron](./baron-dungeon-of-fear-i-i-i) to retrieve the Exit Key and access the dungeon's final Treasure Room to be able to escape.

#### Recommended:
Equipment: Adamantite
Players: 2-4
Plenty of Potions
