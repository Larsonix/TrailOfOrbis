<p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: helvetica, arial, sans-serif;">Major Dungeons</span> adds new custom dungeons to the game. These dungeons are instanced and accessible through the <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">Ancient Portal,</span></strong> which opens after reaching 100 Memories. <span style="color: #999;">In the dungeons, you'll encounter completely <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">New Enemies and Bosses.</span></strong> And as a reward for completing dungeons and killing the final bosses, you can receive <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">Unique Weapons and Armors,</span></strong> which drops at a certain rate, just like in <span style="color: #169179;">Terraria.</span></span></em></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_1c542476-258f-422f-b8d8-c21ab9b4ea78.png" alt="" width="850" height="100%"></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_df2ac92c-2a37-485c-8161-69b5e721984a.png" alt=""></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_dadeb47d-0e94-4818-a712-d530414c6b5e.png" alt=""></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="font-size: 18px;">Dungeon of Fear is the first in the Major Dungeons series. <span style="color: #999;">You'll gradually descend deeper and deeper into the <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">Kingdom of Baal.</span></strong> You'll visit various parts of his kingdom and encounter his deadly minions. <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">If you fail the final boss of a level, you'll have to start over from the beginning,</span></strong> as the one-use crystals required for entering subsequent dungeons after the first drop only from bosses.</span></span></em></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #999;">But there's a workaround: you can buy them from a merchant &#8212; <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">a Nameless God.</span></strong></span></em></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_5f3206c4-a241-4f9c-b446-f2966193637d.png" alt="" width="850" height="100%"></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_077565b3-40ae-4ff7-b0de-17bc766def7b.png" alt="" width="850" height="100%"></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><em><span style="font-size: 18px; color: #999;">This mod adds a new merchant to the game &#8212; <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">a Nameless God.</span></strong> He offers unique <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">Weapons, Armor, and various Artifacts.</span></strong> He only accepts <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">Dark Coins</span></strong> as payment, which can <strong><span style="color: #bf1025;">only be found in dungeons.</span></strong></span></em></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_a6de875f-a35b-4c77-b88b-f728bd64f5b5.png" alt="" width="850" height="100%"></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/1440458/description_1c542476-258f-422f-b8d8-c21ab9b4ea78.png" alt="" width="850" height="100%"></p>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/multiplehud" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">MultipleHUD</a></strong> is required ONLY if you have many mods that add HUDs. You may run into crashes without it. This will be solved in Hytale update 5.</h2>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/stunning-combat" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Stunning Combat</a></strong> is highly recommended, but not required. The dungeon and boss difficulty are based around the parrying and stuns implemented by that mod.</h2>
