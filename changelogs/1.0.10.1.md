## Trail of Orbis v1.0.10.1 - Patch Notes

Hotfix. The skill sanctum was leaking worlds every time you visited it, making portals progressively slower until the server died. That's fixed, plus a full status effect icon pass.

---

### Critical Fix
- **Skill Sanctum World Leak.** Every sanctum visit leaked a world that never got cleaned up. After about 7 visits portals would take 15-30s to open, then eventually crash. Orphan worlds are now cleaned up on startup too, so if you already had the problem it self-heals

### Status Effect Icons
- **Shock and Freeze Show Icons Now.** Lightning essence for shock, ice essence for freeze, both on your HUD when the ailment is active
- **Frost Aura and Frozen Slow Show Icons.** Mobs with the Frost Aura or Frozen modifier now put a debuff icon on you while you're slowed
- **Spawn Protection Has an Icon.** Golden motes with a 60s countdown while you're invincible at realm spawn
- **Conditional Skill Buffs Show Icons.** Every conditional node (Chain Momentum, Fortified, Killing Spree, Ghost, etc.) gets its own unique icon when active. You can finally see what's proccing
- **Poison Countdown Ring Fixed.** When individual poison stacks expired the countdown ring would desync and show the wrong remaining time
- **Frost Icons Don't Blink Anymore.** The icon was disappearing for one frame between reapplications

### Stability
- **HUDs Disappear Less After Teleporting.**
- **Portal "Not In Sync" Error Gone.** Every realm entry was throwing a simulation desync warning, no longer
